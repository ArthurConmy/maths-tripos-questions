Let $X$ and $Y$ be independent random variables, each uniformly distributed on $[0,1]$. Let $U=\min (X, Y)$ and $V=\max (X, Y)$. Show that $\mathbb{E} U=\frac{1}{3}$, and hence find the covariance of $U$ and $V$.
