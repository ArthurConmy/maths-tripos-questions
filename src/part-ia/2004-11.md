Compute the characteristic polynomial of

$$A=\left(\begin{array}{ccc}
3 & -1 & 2 \\
0 & 4-s & 2 s-2 \\
0 & -2 s+2 & 4 s-1
\end{array}\right)$$

Find the eigenvalues and eigenvectors of $A$ for all values of $s$.

For which values of $s$ is $A$ diagonalisable?
