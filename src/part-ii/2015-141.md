Let $K$ be a compact subset of $\mathbb{C}$ with path-connected complement. If $w \notin K$ and $\epsilon>0$, show that there is a polynomial $P$ such that

$$\left|P(z)-\frac{1}{w-z}\right| \leqslant \epsilon$$

for all $z \in K$.
