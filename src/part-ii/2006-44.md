Show that, for $b \neq 0$,

$$\mathcal{P} \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{\cos u}{u^{2}-b^{2}} d u=-\frac{\pi}{2 b} \sin b$$

where $\mathcal{P}$ denotes the Cauchy principal value.
