Find the two leading terms in the asymptotic expansion of the Laplace integral

$$I(x)=\int_{0}^{1} f(t) e^{x t^{4}} d t$$

as $x \rightarrow \infty$, where $f(t)$ is smooth and positive on $[0,1]$.
