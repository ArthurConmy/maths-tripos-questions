Calculate the $L U$ factorization of the matrix

$$A=\left(\begin{array}{rrrr}
3 & 2 & -3 & -3 \\
6 & 3 & -7 & -8 \\
3 & 1 & -6 & -4 \\
-6 & -3 & 9 & 6
\end{array}\right)$$

Use this to evaluate $\operatorname{det}(A)$ and to solve the equation

$$A \mathbf{x}=\mathbf{b}$$

with

$$\mathbf{b}=\left(\begin{array}{r}
3 \\
3 \\
-1 \\
-3
\end{array}\right)$$
