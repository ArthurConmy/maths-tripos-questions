Find an LU factorization of the matrix

$$A=\left(\begin{array}{rrrr}
2 & -1 & 3 & 2 \\
-4 & 3 & -4 & -2 \\
4 & -2 & 3 & 6 \\
-6 & 5 & -8 & 1
\end{array}\right)$$

and use it to solve the linear system $A \mathbf{x}=\mathbf{b}$, where

$$\mathbf{b}=\left(\begin{array}{r}
-2 \\
2 \\
4 \\
11
\end{array}\right)$$
