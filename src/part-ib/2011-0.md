Define differentiability of a function $f: \mathbb{R}^{n} \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$. Let $a>0$ be a constant. For which points $(x, y) \in \mathbb{R}^{2}$ is

$$f(x, y)=|x|^{a}+|x-y|$$

differentiable? Justify your answer.
