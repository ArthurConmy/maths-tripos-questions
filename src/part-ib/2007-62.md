Solve the least squares problem

$$\left[\begin{array}{ll}
1 & 3 \\
0 & 2 \\
0 & 2 \\
0 & 1
\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{l}
x_{1} \\
x_{2}
\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{r}
4 \\
1 \\
4 \\
-1
\end{array}\right]$$

using $Q R$ method with Householder transformation. (A solution using normal equations is not acceptable.)
