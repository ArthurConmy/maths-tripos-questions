Determine the poles of the following functions and calculate their residues there.
(i) $\frac{1}{z^{2}+z^{4}}$,
(ii) $\frac{e^{1 / z^{2}}}{z-1}$,
(iii) $\frac{1}{\sin \left(e^{z}\right)}$.
