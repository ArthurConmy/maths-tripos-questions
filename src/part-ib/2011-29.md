Show that the quaternion group $Q_{8}=\{\pm 1, \pm i, \pm j, \pm k\}$, with $i j=k=-j i$, $i^{2}=j^{2}=k^{2}=-1$, is not isomorphic to the symmetry group $D_{8}$ of the square.
