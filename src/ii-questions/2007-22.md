Define the capacity of a discrete memoryless channel. State Shannon's second coding theorem and use it to show that the discrete memoryless channel with channel matrix

$$\left(\begin{array}{ll}
1 & 0 \\
\frac{1}{2} & \frac{1}{2}
\end{array}\right)$$

has capacity $\log 5-2$.
