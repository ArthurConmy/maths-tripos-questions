Find and classify the fixed points of the system

$$\begin{aligned}
&\dot{x}=x(1-y) \\
&\dot{y}=-y+x^{2} .
\end{aligned}$$

Sketch the phase plane.

What is the $\omega$-limit for the point $(2,-1)$ ? Which points have $(0,0)$ as their $\omega$-limit?
