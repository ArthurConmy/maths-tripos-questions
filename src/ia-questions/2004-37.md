The random variable $X$ has probability density function

$$f(x)= \begin{cases}c x(1-x) & \text { if } 0 \leqslant x \leqslant 1 \\ 0 & \text { otherwise }\end{cases}$$

Determine $c$, and the mean and variance of $X$.
