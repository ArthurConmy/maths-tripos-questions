Let $f: \mathbb{R} \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ be differentiable, and set $g(x)=f^{\prime}(x)$ for $0 \leqslant x \leqslant 1$. Must the Riemann integral of $g$ from 0 to 1 exist?
