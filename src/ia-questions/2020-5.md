Solve the system of differential equations for $x(t), y(t), z(t)$,

$$\begin{aligned}
&\dot{x}=3 z-x \\
&\dot{y}=3 x+2 y-3 z+\cos t-2 \sin t \\
&\dot{z}=3 x-z
\end{aligned}$$

subject to the initial conditions $x(0)=y(0)=0, z(0)=1$.
