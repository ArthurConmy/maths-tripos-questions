Let $a$ be a positive constant. Find the solution to the differential equation

$$\frac{d^{4} y}{d x^{4}}-a^{4} y=\mathrm{e}^{-a x}$$

that satisfies $y(0)=1$ and $y \rightarrow 0$ as $x \rightarrow \infty$.
