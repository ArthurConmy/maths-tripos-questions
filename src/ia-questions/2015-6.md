Find the general solution of the equation

$$2 \frac{d y}{d t}=y-y^{3} .$$

Compute all possible limiting values of $y$ as $t \rightarrow \infty$.

Find a non-zero value of $y(0)$ such that $y(t)=y(0)$ for all $t$.
