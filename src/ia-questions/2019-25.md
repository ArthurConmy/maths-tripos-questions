Show that the series

$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^{2}+n} \quad \text { and } \quad \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{(2 n-1) !}$$

converge. Determine in each case whether the limit is a rational number. Justify your answers.
