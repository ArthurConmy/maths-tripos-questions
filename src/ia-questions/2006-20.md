Find the general solution of the system

$$\begin{aligned}
&\frac{d x}{d t}=5 x+3 y+e^{2 t} \\
&\frac{d y}{d t}=2 x+2 e^{t} \\
&\frac{d z}{d t}=x+y+e^{t}
\end{aligned}$$
