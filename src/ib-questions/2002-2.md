Let $A$ be a complex $4 \times 4$ matrix such that $A^{3}=A^{2}$. What are the possible minimal polynomials of $A$ ? If $A$ is not diagonalisable and $A^{2} \neq 0$, list all possible Jordan normal forms of $A$.
