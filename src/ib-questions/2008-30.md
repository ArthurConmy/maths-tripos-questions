Given that $f(z)$ is an analytic function, show that the mapping $w=f(z)$

(a) preserves angles between smooth curves intersecting at $z$ if $f^{\prime}(z) \neq 0$;

(b) has Jacobian given by $\left|f^{\prime}(z)\right|^{2}$.
