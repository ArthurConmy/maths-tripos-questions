Find a linear change of coordinates such that the quadratic form

$$2 x^{2}+8 x y-6 x z+y^{2}-4 y z+2 z^{2}$$

takes the form

$$\alpha x^{2}+\beta y^{2}+\gamma z^{2}$$

for real numbers $\alpha, \beta$ and $\gamma$.
