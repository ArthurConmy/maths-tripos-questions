Classify the singularities of the following functions at both $z=0$ and at the point at infinity on the extended complex plane:

$$\begin{aligned}
f_{1}(z) &=\frac{e^{z}}{z \sin ^{2} z}, \\
f_{2}(z) &=\frac{1}{z^{2}(1-\cos z)}, \\
f_{3}(z) &=z^{2} \sin (1 / z)
\end{aligned}$$
