Let $\Omega$ be the half-strip in the complex plane,

$$\Omega=\left\{z=x+i y \in \mathbb{C}:-\frac{\pi}{2}<x<\frac{\pi}{2}, \quad y>0\right\}$$

Find a conformal mapping that maps $\Omega$ onto the unit disc.
