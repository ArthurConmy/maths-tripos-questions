Classify the singularities (in the finite complex plane) of the following functions:
(i) $\frac{1}{(\cosh z)^{2}}$;
(ii) $\frac{1}{\cos (1 / z)}$;
(iii) $\frac{1}{\log z} \quad(-\pi<\arg z<\pi)$;
(iv) $\frac{z^{\frac{1}{2}}-1}{\sin \pi z} \quad(-\pi<\arg z<\pi)$.
