Let $G$ be a group and $P$ a subgroup.

(a) Define the normaliser $N_{G}(P)$.

(b) Suppose that $K \triangleleft G$ and $P$ is a Sylow $p$-subgroup of $K$. Using Sylow's second theorem, prove that $G=N_{G}(P) K$.
