Prove by using the Cauchy theorem that if $f$ is analytic in the open disc $\Omega=\{z \in \mathbb{C}:|z|<1\}$ then there exists a function $g$, analytic in $\Omega$, such that $g^{\prime}(z)=f(z)$, $z \in \Omega$.
