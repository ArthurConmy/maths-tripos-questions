Consider the function $\phi$ defined by

$$\phi(b)=\inf \left\{x^{2}+y^{4}: x+2 y=b\right\}$$

Use the Lagrangian sufficiency theorem to evaluate $\phi(3)$. Compute the derivative $\phi^{\prime}(3)$.
