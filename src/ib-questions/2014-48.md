Using the method of characteristics, solve the differential equation

$$-y \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+x \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}=0$$

where $x, y \in \mathbb{R}$ and $u=\cos y^{2}$ on $x=0, y \geqslant 0$.
