What is the adjugate of an $n \times n$ matrix $A$ ? How is it related to $A^{-1}$ ? Suppose all the entries of $A$ are integers. Show that all the entries of $A^{-1}$ are integers if and only if $\operatorname{det} A=\pm 1$.
