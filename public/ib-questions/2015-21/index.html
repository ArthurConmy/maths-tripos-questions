A fluid layer of depth h1h_{1}h1​ and dynamic viscosity μ1\mu_{1}μ1​ is located underneath a fluid layer of depth h2h_{2}h2​ and dynamic viscosity μ2\mu_{2}μ2​. The total fluid system of depth h=h1+h2h=h_{1}+h_{2}h=h1​+h2​ is positioned between a stationary rigid plate at y=0y=0y=0 and a rigid plate at y=hy=hy=h moving with speed U=Ux^\mathbf{U}=U \hat{\mathbf{x}}U=Ux^, where UUU is constant. Ignore the effects of gravity.

(i) Using dimensional analysis only, and the fact that the stress should be linear in UUU, derive the expected form of the shear stress acted by the fluid on the plate at y=0y=0y=0 as a function of U,h1,h2,μ1U, h_{1}, h_{2}, \mu_{1}U,h1​,h2​,μ1​ and μ2\mu_{2}μ2​.
