#Metric and Topological Spaces

(i) XXX is a Hausdorff space.

(ii) The diagonal Δ={(x,x):x∈X}\Delta=\{(x, x): x \in X\}Δ={(x,x):x∈X} is a closed subset of X×XX \times XX×X, in the product topology.
