#Groups, Rings and Modules
2015

(i) Give the definition of a ppp-Sylow subgroup of a group.

(ii) Let GGG be a group of order 2835=34⋅5⋅72835=3^{4} \cdot 5 \cdot 72835=34⋅5⋅7. Show that there are at most two possibilities for the number of 3-Sylow subgroups, and give the possible numbers of 3-Sylow subgroups.

(iii) Continuing with a group GGG of order 2835 , show that GGG is not simple.
