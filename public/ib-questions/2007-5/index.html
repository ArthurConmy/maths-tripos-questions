#Linear Algebra
2007

Suppose that α:V→W\alpha: V \rightarrow Wα:V→W is a linear map of finite-dimensional complex vector spaces. What is the dual map α∗\alpha^{*}α∗ of the dual vector spaces?

Suppose that we choose bases of V,WV, WV,W and take the corresponding dual bases of the dual vector spaces. What is the relation between the matrices that represent α\alphaα and α∗\alpha^{*}α∗ with respect to these bases? Justify your answer.
