x′=xcosh⁡ϕ−ctsinh⁡ϕct′=−xsinh⁡ϕ+ctcosh⁡ϕ\begin{aligned} x^{\prime} &=x \cosh \phi-c t \sinh \phi \\ c t^{\prime} &=-x \sinh \phi+c t \cosh \phi \end{aligned}x′ct′​=xcoshϕ−ctsinhϕ=−xsinhϕ+ctcoshϕ​

x′+ct′=e−ϕ(x+ct)x′−ct′=eϕ(x−ct)\begin{aligned} &x^{\prime}+c t^{\prime}=e^{-\phi}(x+c t) \\ &x^{\prime}-c t^{\prime}=e^{\phi}(x-c t) \end{aligned}​x′+ct′=e−ϕ(x+ct)x′−ct′=eϕ(x−ct)​
