3.II.14 F3 . \mathrm{II} . 14 \mathrm{~F} \quad3.II.14 F

#Groups, Rings and Modules
2004

Let LLL be the group Z3\mathbb{Z}^{3}Z3 consisting of 3-dimensional row vectors with integer components. Let MMM be the subgroup of LLL generated by the three vectors

u=(1,2,3),v=(2,3,1),w=(3,1,2). u=(1,2,3), v=(2,3,1), w=(3,1,2) \text {. } u=(1,2,3),v=(2,3,1),w=(3,1,2). 

(i) What is the index of MMM in LLL ?

(ii) Prove that MMM is not a direct summand of LLL.

(iii) Is the subgroup NNN generated by uuu and vvv a direct summand of LLL ?

(iv) What is the structure of the quotient group L/ML / ML/M ?
