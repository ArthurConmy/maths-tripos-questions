#Special Relativity
2002

Write down the formulae for a Lorentz transformation with velocity vvv taking one set of co-ordinates (t,x)(t, x)(t,x) to another (t′,x′)\left(t^{\prime}, x^{\prime}\right)(t′,x′).

Imagine you observe a train travelling past Cambridge station at a relativistic speed uuu. Someone standing still on the train throws a ball in the direction the train is moving, with speed vvv. How fast do you observe the ball to be moving? Justify your answer.
