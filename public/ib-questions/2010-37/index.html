#Linear Algebra
2010

Suppose that ϕ\phiϕ is an endomorphism of a finite-dimensional complex vector space.

(i) Show that if λ\lambdaλ is an eigenvalue of ϕ\phiϕ, then λ2\lambda^{2}λ2 is an eigenvalue of ϕ2\phi^{2}ϕ2.

(ii) Show conversely that if μ\muμ is an eigenvalue of ϕ2\phi^{2}ϕ2, then there is an eigenvalue λ\lambdaλ of ϕ\phiϕ with λ2=μ\lambda^{2}=\muλ2=μ.
