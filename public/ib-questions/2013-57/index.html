#Metric and Topological Spaces
2013

Let XXX be a topological space. A connected component of XXX means an equivalence class with respect to the equivalence relation on XXX defined as:

x∼y⟺x,y belong to some connected subspace of X.x \sim y \Longleftrightarrow x, y \text { belong to some connected subspace of } X . x∼y⟺x,y belong to some connected subspace of X.

(i) Show that every connected component is a connected and closed subset of XXX.

(ii) If X,YX, YX,Y are topological spaces and X×YX \times YX×Y is the product space, show that every connected component of X×YX \times YX×Y is a direct product of connected components of XXX and YYY.
