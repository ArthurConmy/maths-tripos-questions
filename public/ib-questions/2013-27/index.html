Paper 3, Section II, F

#Geometry
2013

Show that the set of all straight lines in R2\mathbb{R}^{2}R2 admits the structure of an abstract smooth surface SSS. Show that SSS is an open Möbius band (i.e. the Möbius band without its boundary circle), and deduce that SSS admits a Riemannian metric with vanishing Gauss curvature.

Show that there is no metric d:S×S→R⩾0d: S \times S \rightarrow \mathbb{R}_{\geqslant 0}d:S×S→R⩾0​, in the sense of metric spaces, which

  1. induces the locally Euclidean topology on SSS constructed above;

  2. is invariant under the natural action on SSS of the group of translations of R2\mathbb{R}^{2}R2.

Show that the set of great circles on the two-dimensional sphere admits the structure of a smooth surface S′S^{\prime}S′. Is S′S^{\prime}S′ homeomorphic to SSS ? Does S′S^{\prime}S′ admit a Riemannian metric with vanishing Gauss curvature? Briefly justify your answers.
