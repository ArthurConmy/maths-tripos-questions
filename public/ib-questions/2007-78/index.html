#Markov Chains

For a Markov chain with state space SSS, define what is meant by the following:

(i) states i,j∈Si, j \in Si,j∈S communicate;

(ii) state i∈Si \in Si∈S is recurrent.

Prove that communication is an equivalence relation on SSS and that if two states i,ji, ji,j communicate and iii is recurrent then jjj is recurrent.
