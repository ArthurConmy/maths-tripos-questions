#Linear Algebra

Let VVV and WWW be finite dimensional real vector spaces and let T:V→WT: V \rightarrow WT:V→W be a linear map. Define the dual space V∗V^{*}V∗ and the dual map T∗T^{*}T∗. Show that there is an isomorphism ι:V→(V∗)∗\iota: V \rightarrow\left(V^{*}\right)^{*}ι:V→(V∗)∗ which is canonical, in the sense that ι∘S=(S∗)∗∘ι\iota \circ S=\left(S^{*}\right)^{*} \circ \iotaι∘S=(S∗)∗∘ι for any automorphism SSS of VVV.

Now let WWW be an inner product space. Use the inner product to show that there is an injective map from im TTT to im⁡T∗\operatorname{im} T^{*}imT∗. Deduce that the row rank of a matrix is equal to its column rank.
