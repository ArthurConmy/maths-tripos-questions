2.II.11E

#Groups, Rings and Modules
2006

(i) Prove the first Sylow theorem, that a finite group of order pnrp^{n} rpnr with ppp prime and ppp not dividing the integer rrr has a subgroup of order pnp^{n}pn.

(ii) State the remaining Sylow theorems.

(iii) Show that if ppp and qqq are distinct primes then no group of order pqp qpq is simple.
