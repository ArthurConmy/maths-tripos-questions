2.I.1E

#Linear Algebra
2008

Suppose that VVV and WWW are finite-dimensional vector spaces over R\mathbb{R}R. What does it mean to say that ψ:V→W\psi: V \rightarrow Wψ:V→W is a linear map? State the rank-nullity formula. Using it, or otherwise, prove that a linear map ψ:V→V\psi: V \rightarrow Vψ:V→V is surjective if, and only if, it is injective.

Suppose that ψ:V→V\psi: V \rightarrow Vψ:V→V is a linear map which has a right inverse, that is to say there is a linear map ϕ:V→V\phi: V \rightarrow Vϕ:V→V such that ψϕ=idV\psi \phi=\mathrm{id}_{V}ψϕ=idV​, the identity map. Show that ϕψ=idV\phi \psi=\mathrm{id}_{V}ϕψ=idV​.

Suppose that AAA and BBB are two n×nn \times nn×n matrices over R\mathbb{R}R such that AB=IA B=IAB=I. Prove that BA=IB A=IBA=I.
