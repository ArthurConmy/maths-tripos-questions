2.II.11C

#Groups, Rings and Modules
2005

Let AAA be the abelian group generated by two elements x,yx, yx,y, subject to the relation 6x+9y=06 x+9 y=06x+9y=0. Give a rigorous explanation of this statement by defining AAA as an appropriate quotient of a free abelian group of rank 2. Prove that AAA itself is not a free abelian group, and determine the exact structure of AAA.
