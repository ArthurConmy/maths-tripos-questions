#Geometry
2007

Let lll be a line in the Euclidean plane R2\mathbf{R}^{2}R2 and PPP a point on lll. Denote by ρ\rhoρ the reflection in lll and by τ\tauτ the rotation through an angle α\alphaα about PPP. Describe, in terms of l,Pl, Pl,P, and α\alphaα, a line fixed by the composition τρ\tau \rhoτρ and show that τρ\tau \rhoτρ is a reflection.
