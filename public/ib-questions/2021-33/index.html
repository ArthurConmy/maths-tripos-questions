Paper 3, Section II, 10G

#Groups, Rings and Modules
2021

Let ppp be a non-zero element of a Principal Ideal Domain RRR. Show that the following are equivalent:

(i) ppp is prime;

(ii) ppp is irreducible;

(iii) (p)(p)(p) is a maximal ideal of RRR;

(iv) R/(p)R /(p)R/(p) is a field;

(v) R/(p)R /(p)R/(p) is an Integral Domain.

Let RRR be a Principal Ideal Domain, SSS an Integral Domain and ϕ:R→S\phi: R \rightarrow Sϕ:R→S a surjective ring homomorphism. Show that either ϕ\phiϕ is an isomorphism or SSS is a field.

Show that if RRR is a commutative ring and R[X]R[X]R[X] is a Principal Ideal Domain, then RRR is a field.

Let RRR be an Integral Domain in which every two non-zero elements have a highest common factor. Show that in RRR every irreducible element is prime.
