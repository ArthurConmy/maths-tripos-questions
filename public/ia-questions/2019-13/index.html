#Dynamics and Relativity

Galileo releases a cannonball of mass mmm from the top of the leaning tower of Pisa, a vertical height hhh above the ground. Ignoring the rotation of the Earth but assuming that the cannonball experiences a quadratic drag force whose magnitude is γv2\gamma v^{2}γv2 (where vvv is the speed of the cannonball), find the time for it to hit the ground in terms of h,m,γh, m, \gammah,m,γ and ggg, the acceleration due to gravity. [You may assume that ggg is constant.]
