(i) State and prove the Orbit-Stabilizer Theorem.

Show that if GGG is a finite group of order nnn, then GGG is isomorphic to a subgroup of the symmetric group SnS_{n}Sn​.

Show that the homomorphism G→Sym⁡(X)G \rightarrow \operatorname{Sym}(X)G→Sym(X) given by the action is injective if and only if the intersection of all the conjugates of HHH equals {e}\{e\}{e}.

(iii) Let Q8Q_{8}Q8​ denote the quaternion group of order 8 . Show that for every n<8,Q8n<8, Q_{8}n<8,Q8​ is not isomorphic to a subgroup of SnS_{n}Sn​.
