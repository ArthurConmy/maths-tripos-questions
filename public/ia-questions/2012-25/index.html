Paper 4, Section I, 2D2 \mathrm{D}2D

#Numbers and Sets
2012

What is an equivalence relation on a set XXX ? If RRR is an equivalence relation on XXX, what is an equivalence class of RRR ? Prove that the equivalence classes of RRR form a partition of XXX.

Let RRR and SSS be equivalence relations on a set XXX. Which of the following are always equivalence relations? Give proofs or counterexamples as appropriate.

(i) The relation VVV on XXX given by xVyx V yxVy if both xRyx R yxRy and xSyx S yxSy.

(ii) The relation WWW on XXX given by xWyx W yxWy if xRyx R yxRy or xSyx S yxSy.
