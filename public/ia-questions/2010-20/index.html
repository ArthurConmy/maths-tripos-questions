Paper 3, Section II, D

#Groups
2010

(i) State the orbit-stabilizer theorem.

Let GGG be the group of rotations of the cube, XXX the set of faces. Identify the stabilizer of a face, and hence compute the order of GGG.

Describe the orbits of GGG on the set X×XX \times XX×X of pairs of faces.

(ii) Define what it means for a subgroup NNN of GGG to be normal. Show that GGG has a normal subgroup of order 4 .
