1.II.10E

Groups, Rings and Modules
Part IB, 2006

Find all subgroups of indices 2,3,42,3,42,3,4 and 5 in the alternating group A5A_{5}A5​ on 5 letters. You may use any general result that you choose, provided that you state it clearly, but you must justify your answers.

[You may take for granted the fact that A4A_{4}A4​ has no subgroup of index 2.]
