1.II.17F

Quadratic Mathematics
Part IB, 2002

Let AAA and BBB be n×nn \times nn×n real symmetric matrices, such that the quadratic form xTAx\mathbf{x}^{T} A \mathbf{x}xTAx is positive definite. Show that it is possible to find an invertible matrix PPP such that PTAP=IP^{T} A P=IPTAP=I and PTBPP^{T} B PPTBP is diagonal. Show also that the diagonal entries of the matrix PTBPP^{T} B PPTBP may be calculated directly from AAA and BBB, without finding the matrix PPP. If
