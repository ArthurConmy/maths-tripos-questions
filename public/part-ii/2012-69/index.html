Paper 3, Section I, G

Geometry and Groups
Part II, 2012

Let AAA be a Möbius transformation acting on the Riemann sphere. Show that, if AAA is not loxodromic, then there is a disc Δ\DeltaΔ in the Riemann sphere with A(Δ)=ΔA(\Delta)=\DeltaA(Δ)=Δ. Describe all such discs for each Möbius transformation AAA.

Hence, or otherwise, show that the group GGG of Möbius transformations generated by

A:z↦iz and B:z↦2zA: z \mapsto i z \quad \text { and } \quad B: z \mapsto 2 z A:z↦iz and B:z↦2z

does not map any disc onto itself.

Describe the set of points of the Riemann sphere at which GGG acts discontinuously. What is the quotient of this set by the action of GGG ?
