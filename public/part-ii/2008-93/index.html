3.II.23H

Differential Geometry
Part II, 2008

Let S⊂R3S \subset \mathbb{R}^{3}S⊂R3 be a surface.

(a) Define the Gauss Map, principal curvatures kik_{i}ki​, Gaussian curvature KKK and mean curvature HHH. State the Theorema Egregium.

(b) Define what is meant for SSS to be minimal. Prove that if SSS is minimal, then K⩽0K \leqslant 0K⩽0. Give an example of a minimal surface whose Gaussian curvature is not identically 0 , justifying your answer.

(c) Does there exist a compact minimal surface S⊂R3S \subset \mathbb{R}^{3}S⊂R3 ? Justify your answer.
