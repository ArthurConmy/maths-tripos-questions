Linear Analysis
Part II, 2018

(a) Let XXX be a normed vector space and let YYY be a Banach space. Show that the space of bounded linear operators B(X,Y)\mathcal{B}(X, Y)B(X,Y) is a Banach space.

(b) Let XXX and YYY be Banach spaces, and let D⊂XD \subset XD⊂X be a dense linear subspace. Prove that a bounded linear map T:D→YT: D \rightarrow YT:D→Y can be extended uniquely to a bounded linear map T:X→YT: X \rightarrow YT:X→Y with the same operator norm. Is the claim also true if one of XXX and YYY is not complete?
