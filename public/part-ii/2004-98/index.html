B3.7

Algebraic Topology
Part II, 2004

A finite simplicial complex KKK is the union of subcomplexes LLL and MMM. Describe the Mayer-Vietoris exact sequence that relates the homology groups of KKK to those of LLL, MMM and L∩ML \cap ML∩M. Define all the homomorphisms in the sequence, proving that they are well-defined (a proof of exactness is not required).

A surface XXX is constructed by identifying together (by means of a homeomorphism) the boundaries of two Möbius strips YYY and ZZZ. Assuming relevant triangulations exist, determine the homology groups of XXX.
