Paper 1, Section II, H

Algebraic Topology
Part II, 2015

State carefully a version of the Seifert-van Kampen theorem for a cover of a space by two closed sets.

Let XXX be the space obtained by gluing together a Möbius band MMM and a torus T=S1×S1T=S^{1} \times S^{1}T=S1×S1 along a homeomorphism of the boundary of MMM with S1×{1}⊂TS^{1} \times\{1\} \subset TS1×{1}⊂T. Find a presentation for the fundamental group of XXX, and hence show that it is infinite and non-abelian.
