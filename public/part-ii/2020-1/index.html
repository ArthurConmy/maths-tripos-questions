Algebraic Geometry
Part II, 2020

Let kkk be an algebraically closed field of characteristic not equal to 2 and let V⊂Pk3V \subset \mathbb{P}_{k}^{3}V⊂Pk3​ be a nonsingular quadric surface.

(a) Prove that VVV is birational to Pk2\mathbb{P}_{k}^{2}Pk2​.

(b) Prove that there exists a pair of disjoint lines on VVV.

(c) Prove that the affine variety W=V(xyz−1)⊂Ak3W=\mathbb{V}(x y z-1) \subset \mathbb{A}_{k}^{3}W=V(xyz−1)⊂Ak3​ does not contain any lines.
