Paper 3, Section II, I

Galois Theory
Part II, 2017

(a) Let FFF be a finite field of characteristic ppp. Show that FFF is a finite Galois extension of the field FpF_{p}Fp​ of ppp elements, and that the Galois group of FFF over FpF_{p}Fp​ is cyclic.

(b) Find the Galois groups of the following polynomials:

(i) t4+1t^{4}+1t4+1 over F3F_{3}F3​.

(ii) t3−t−2t^{3}-t-2t3−t−2 over F5F_{5}F5​.

(iii) t4−1t^{4}-1t4−1 over F7F_{7}F7​.
