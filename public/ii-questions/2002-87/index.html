#Representation Theory

State and prove Schur's Lemma. Deduce that the centre of a finite group GGG with a faithful irreducible complex representation ρ\rhoρ is cyclic and that Z(ρ(G))Z(\rho(G))Z(ρ(G)) consists of scalar transformations.

Let GGG be the subgroup of order 18 of the symmetric group S6S_{6}S6​ given by

G=⟨(123),(456),(23)(56)⟩G=\langle(123),(456),(23)(56)\rangle G=⟨(123),(456),(23)(56)⟩

Show that GGG has a normal subgroup of order 9 and four normal subgroups of order 3 . By considering quotients, show that GGG has two representations of dimension 1 and four inequivalent irreducible representations of degree 2 . Deduce that GGG has no faithful irreducible complex representations.

Show finally that if GGG is a finite group with trivial centre and HHH is a subgroup of GGG with non-trivial centre, then any faithful representation of GGG is reducible on restriction to HHH.
