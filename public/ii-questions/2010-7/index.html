#Algebraic Topology

State the Snake Lemma. Explain how to define the boundary map which appears in it, and check that it is well-defined. Derive the Mayer-Vietoris sequence from the Snake Lemma.

Given a chain complex CCC, let A⊂CA \subset CA⊂C be the span of all elements in CCC with grading greater than or equal to nnn, and let B⊂CB \subset CB⊂C be the span of all elements in CCC with grading less than nnn. Give a short exact sequence of chain complexes relating A,BA, BA,B, and CCC. What is the boundary map in the corresponding long exact sequence?
