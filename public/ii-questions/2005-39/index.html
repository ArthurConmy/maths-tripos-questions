2.II.14B

#Dynamical Systems
2005

Prove that if a continuous map FFF of an interval into itself has a periodic orbit of period three then it also has periodic orbits of least period nnn for all positive integers nnn.

Explain briefly why there must be at least two periodic orbits of least period 5.5 .5.

[You may assume without proof:

(i) If UUU and VVV are non-empty closed bounded intervals such that V⊆F(U)V \subseteq F(U)V⊆F(U) then there is a closed bounded interval K⊆UK \subseteq UK⊆U such that F(K)=VF(K)=VF(K)=V.

(ii) The Intermediate Value Theorem.]
