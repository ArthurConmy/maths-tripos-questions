A4.9

#Graph Theory
2004

Write an essay on trees. You should include a proof of Cayley's result on the number of labelled trees of order nnn.

Let GGG be a graph of order n≥2n \geq 2n≥2. Which of the following statements are equivalent to the statement that GGG is a tree? Give a proof or counterexample in each case.

(a) GGG is acyclic and e(G)≥n−1e(G) \geq n-1e(G)≥n−1.

(b) GGG is connected and e(G)≤n−1e(G) \leq n-1e(G)≤n−1.

(c) GGG is connected, triangle-free and has at least two leaves.

(d) GGG has the same degree sequence as TTT, for some tree TTT.
