Paper 1, Section II, I

#Graph Theory
2014

Show that a graph is bipartite if and only if all of its cycles are of even length.

Show that a bridgeless plane graph is bipartite if and only if all of its faces are of even length.

Let GGG be an Eulerian plane graph. Show that the faces of GGG can be coloured with two colours so that no two contiguous faces have the same colour. Deduce that it is possible to assign a direction to each edge of GGG in such a way that the edges around each face form a directed cycle.
