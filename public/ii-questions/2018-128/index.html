(a) Suppose that Alice and Bob are distantly separated in space and each has one qubit of the 2-qubit state ∣ϕ+⟩=12(∣00⟩+∣11⟩)\left|\phi^{+}\right\rangle=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(|00\rangle+|11\rangle)∣ϕ+⟩=2​1​(∣00⟩+∣11⟩). Alice has a 1-qubit state ∣α⟩|\alpha\rangle∣α⟩ (whose identity is unknown to her) which she wants to communicate to Bob. Show how this can be achieved using only the operational resources, listed above, that they have available.

Suppose now that a third party, called Charlie, joins Alice and Bob. They are all mutually distantly separated in space and each holds one qubit of the 3-qubit state

∣γ⟩=12(∣000⟩+∣111⟩).|\gamma\rangle=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(|000\rangle+|111\rangle) . ∣γ⟩=2​1​(∣000⟩+∣111⟩).
