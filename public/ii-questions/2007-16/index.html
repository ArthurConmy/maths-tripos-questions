4.I .3G. 3 \mathrm{G}.3G

#Geometry of Group Actions
2007

Let Γ\GammaΓ be a circle on the Riemann sphere. Explain what it means to say that two points of the sphere are inverse points for the circle Γ\GammaΓ. Show that, for each point zzz on the Riemann sphere, there is a unique point z′z^{\prime}z′ with z,z′z, z^{\prime}z,z′ inverse points. Define inversion in Γ\GammaΓ.

Prove that the composition of an even number of inversions is a Möbius transformation.
