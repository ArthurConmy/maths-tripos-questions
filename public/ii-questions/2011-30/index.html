#Coding and Cryptography
2011

Define a cyclic code. Show that there is a bijection between the cyclic codes of length nnn and the factors of Xn−1X^{n}-1Xn−1 over the field F2\mathbb{F}_{2}F2​ of order 2 .

What is meant by saying that α\alphaα is a primitive nnnth root of unity in a finite field extension KKK of F2\mathbb{F}_{2}F2​ ? What is meant by saying that CCC is a BCH code of length nnn with defining set {α,α2,…,αδ−1}\left\{\alpha, \alpha^{2}, \ldots, \alpha^{\delta-1}\right\}{α,α2,…,αδ−1} ? Show that such a code has minimum distance at least δ\deltaδ.

Suppose that KKK is a finite field extension of F2\mathbb{F}_{2}F2​ in which X7−1X^{7}-1X7−1 factorises into linear factors. Show that if β\betaβ is a root of X3+X2+1X^{3}+X^{2}+1X3+X2+1 then β\betaβ is a primitive 7 th root of unity and β2\beta^{2}β2 is also a root of X3+X2+1X^{3}+X^{2}+1X3+X2+1. Quoting any further results that you need show that the BCH\mathrm{BCH}BCH code of length 7 with defining set {β,β2}\left\{\beta, \beta^{2}\right\}{β,β2} is the Hamming code.

[Results on the Vandermonde determinant may be used without proof provided they are quoted correctly.]
