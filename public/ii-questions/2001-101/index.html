B3.8

#Hilbert Spaces
2001

Let TTT be a bounded linear operator on a Hilbert space HHH. Define what it means to say that TTT is (i) compact, and (ii) Fredholm. What is the index, ind (T)(T)(T), of a Fredholm operator TTT ?

Let S,TS, TS,T be bounded linear operators on HHH. Prove that SSS and TTT are Fredholm if and only if both STS TST and TST STS are Fredholm. Prove also that if SSS is invertible and TTT is Fredholm then ind⁡(ST)=ind⁡(TS)=ind⁡(T)\operatorname{ind}(S T)=\operatorname{ind}(T S)=\operatorname{ind}(T)ind(ST)=ind(TS)=ind(T).

Let KKK be a compact linear operator on HHH. Prove that I+KI+KI+K is Fredholm with index zero.
