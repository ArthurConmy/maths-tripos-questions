Are the following statements true or false? Justify your answers.

[You may quote standard facts about planar and non-planar graphs, provided that they are clearly stated.]

(i) If GGG is a graph with χ(G)⩽4\chi(G) \leqslant 4χ(G)⩽4 then GGG is planar.

(ii) If GGG is a connected graph with average degree at most 2.012.012.01 then GGG is planar.

(iii) If GGG is a connected graph with average degree at most 2 then GGG is planar.
