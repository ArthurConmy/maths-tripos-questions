#Galois Theory
2005

(i) Let KKK be the splitting field of the polynomial

x4−4x2−1x^{4}-4 x^{2}-1 x4−4x2−1

over Q\mathbb{Q}Q. Show that [K:Q]=8[K: \mathbb{Q}]=8[K:Q]=8, and hence show that the Galois group of K/QK / \mathbb{Q}K/Q is the dihedral group of order 8 .

(ii) Let LLL be the splitting field of the polynomial

x4−4x2+1x^{4}-4 x^{2}+1 x4−4x2+1

over Q\mathbb{Q}Q. Show that [L:Q]=4[L: \mathbb{Q}]=4[L:Q]=4. Show that the Galois group of L/QL / \mathbb{Q}L/Q is C2×C2C_{2} \times C_{2}C2​×C2​.
