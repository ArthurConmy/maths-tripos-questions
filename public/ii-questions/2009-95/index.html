#Number Fields
2009

Suppose that KKK is a number field of degree n=r+2sn=r+2 sn=r+2s, where KKK has exactly real embeddings.

Show that the group of units in OK\mathcal{O}_{K}OK​ is a finitely generated abelian group of rank at most r+s−1r+s-1r+s−1. Identify the torsion subgroup in terms of roots of unity.

[General results about discrete subgroups of a Euclidean real vector space may be used without proof, provided that they are stated clearly.]

Find all the roots of unity in Q(11)\mathbb{Q}(\sqrt{11})Q(11​).
