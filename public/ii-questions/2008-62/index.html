#Logic and Set Theory
2008

What is a transitive set? Show that if xxx is transitive then so are the union ⋃x\bigcup x⋃x and the power set PxP xPx of xxx. If ⋃x\bigcup x⋃x is transitive, is xxx transitive? If PxP xPx is transitive, is xxx transitive? Justify your answers.

What is the transitive closure of a set? Show that any set xxx has a transitive closure TC(x)T C(x)TC(x).

Suppose that xxx has rank α\alphaα. What is the rank of PxP xPx ? What is the rank of TC(x)T C(x)TC(x) ?

[You may use standard properties of rank.]
