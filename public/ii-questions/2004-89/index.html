B3.5

#Representation Theory
2004

Compute the character table for the group A5A_{5}A5​ of even permutations of five elements. You may wish to follow the steps below.

(a) List the conjugacy classes in A5A_{5}A5​ and their orders.

(b) A5A_{5}A5​ acts on C5\mathbb{C}^{5}C5 by permuting the standard basis vectors. Show that C5\mathbb{C}^{5}C5 splits as C⊕V\mathbb{C} \oplus VC⊕V, where C\mathbb{C}C is the trivial 1-dimensional representation and VVV is irreducible.

(c) By using the formula for the character of the symmetric square S2VS^{2} VS2V,

χS2V(g)=12[χV(g)2+χV(g2)]\chi_{S^{2} V}(g)=\frac{1}{2}\left[\chi_{V}(g)^{2}+\chi_{V}\left(g^{2}\right)\right] χS2V​(g)=21​[χV​(g)2+χV​(g2)]

decompose S2VS^{2} VS2V to produce a 5-dimensional, irreducible representation, and find its character.

(d) Show that the exterior square Λ2V\Lambda^{2} VΛ2V decomposes into two distinct irreducibles and compute their characters, to complete the character table of A5A_{5}A5​.

[Hint: You can save yourself some computational effort if you can explain why the automorphism of A5A_{5}A5​, defined by conjugation by a transposition in S5S_{5}S5​, must swap the two summands of Λ2V\Lambda^{2} VΛ2V.]
