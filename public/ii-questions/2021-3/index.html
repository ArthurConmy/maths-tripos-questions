Paper 4, Section II, I

#Algebraic Geometry
2021

Let CCC be a smooth irreducible projective algebraic curve over an algebraically closed field.

Let DDD be an effective divisor on CCC. Prove that the vector space L(D)L(D)L(D) of rational functions with poles bounded by DDD is finite dimensional.

Let DDD and EEE be linearly equivalent divisors on CCC. Exhibit an isomorphism between the vector spaces L(D)L(D)L(D) and L(E)L(E)L(E).

What is a canonical divisor on CCC ? State the Riemann-Roch theorem and use it to calculate the degree of a canonical divisor in terms of the genus of CCC.

Prove that the canonical divisor on a smooth cubic plane curve is linearly equivalent to the zero divisor.
