#Hilbert Spaces

[You may assume, without proof, that H=E⊕E⊥H=E \oplus E^{\perp}H=E⊕E⊥, for every closed subspace EEE of HHH.]

Define a normal operator T∈B(H)T \in \mathcal{B}(H)T∈B(H). Prove that TTT is normal if and only if ∥Tx∥=∥T∗x∥\|T x\|=\left\|T^{*} x\right\|∥Tx∥=∥T∗x∥ for every x∈Hx \in Hx∈H. Deduce that every point in the spectrum of a normal operator TTT is an approximate eigenvalue of TTT.
