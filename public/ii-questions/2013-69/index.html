Paper 3, Section I, 3G3 G3G

#Geometry and Groups
2013

Let Λ\LambdaΛ be a rank 2 lattice in the Euclidean plane. Show that the group GGG of all Euclidean isometries of the plane that map Λ\LambdaΛ onto itself is a discrete group. List the possible sizes of the point groups for GGG and give examples to show that point groups of these sizes do arise.

[You may quote any standard results without proof.]
