#Galois Theory

Let M/KM / KM/K be a finite Galois extension of fields. Explain what is meant by the Galois correspondence between subfields of MMM containing KKK and subgroups of Gal⁡(M/K)\operatorname{Gal}(M / K)Gal(M/K). Show that if K⊂L⊂MK \subset L \subset MK⊂L⊂M then Gal⁡(M/L)\operatorname{Gal}(M / L)Gal(M/L) is a normal subgroup of Gal⁡(M/K)\operatorname{Gal}(M / K)Gal(M/K) if and only if L/KL / KL/K is normal. What is Gal⁡(L/K)\operatorname{Gal}(L / K)Gal(L/K) in this case?

Let MMM be the splitting field of X4−3X^{4}-3X4−3 over Q\mathbb{Q}Q. Prove that Gal⁡(M/Q)\operatorname{Gal}(M / \mathbb{Q})Gal(M/Q) is isomorphic to the dihedral group of order 8. Hence determine all subfields of MMM, expressing each in the form Q(x)\mathbb{Q}(x)Q(x) for suitable x∈Mx \in Mx∈M.
