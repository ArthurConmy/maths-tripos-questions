2.II.10F

Probability
Part IA, 2008

AAA and BBB play a series of games. The games are independent, and each is won by AAA with probability ppp and by BBB with probability 1−p1-p1−p. The players stop when the number of wins by one player is three greater than the number of wins by the other player. The player with the greater number of wins is then declared overall winner.

(i) Find the probability that exactly 5 games are played.

(ii) Find the probability that AAA is the overall winner.
